Essential Attributes of the Anchor Tag

Anchor Tag

Links are fundamental to navigating the web. In HTML, links are created using the <a> tag, also known as the Anchor tag.

Essential Attributes of the Anchor Tag

href attribute: Defines the URL the link points to.
target attribute: Specifies where to open the linked document.

Google

Google _blank: Opens the linked document in a new window or tab.

Google _self: Opens document in the same window or tab (default behavior).

Google _top: _top: Opens document in the full body of the window.
